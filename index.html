Who We Are

We are Nafsi-Friq Events, a design and branding agency with partners worldwide. We design thoughtful digital experiences and beautiful brand aesthetics.

We offer a range of services in the fields of digital marketing, events sponsoring, and event planning. From crafting innovative digital marketing strategies to organizing unforgettable events and providing event sponsorship solutions, our team is committed to helping businesses grow and succeed. Whether you're looking to connect with your target audience, create memorable experiences, or bring your vision to life, we have the expertise and experience to make it happen. Let us be your partner in success.

Define

Our expertise in event sponsoring allows us to expertly define sponsorship parameters, offering clear and enticing benefits to potential sponsors. By partnering with us, you can rest assured that your event will be a resounding success, attracting the right audience and securing valuable sponsorships. Trust our skill in defining the perfect strategy for your event, and experience the power of precise planning and successful collaboration.

Design

Whether it's event branding, stage setup, or decor, we excel at creating visually stunning and immersive environments that leave a lasting impression on attendees. We understand that design goes beyond aesthetics; it's about creating an atmosphere that aligns with your event's objectives and enhances the overall experience. Trust us to elevate your event with our exceptional design expertise and create an unforgettable experience that will exceed your expectations.

Build

We specialize in helping businesses build a strong digital presence through strategic digital marketing and design solutions. Our team of experts is dedicated to creating compelling and effective digital marketing campaigns that resonate with your target audience. From developing a comprehensive digital marketing strategy to implementing cutting-edge techniques, we will help you build brand awareness, drive traffic, and achieve your business goals.

Launch

Our expert team will work closely with you to develop a comprehensive launch strategy that encompasses market research, strategic positioning, and compelling messaging. Leveraging our expertise in marketing, event coordination, and digital platforms, we will generate excitement and drive initial adoption for your offering. Trust us to guide you through a successful launch that captures the attention of your target audience and propels your business to new heights.
